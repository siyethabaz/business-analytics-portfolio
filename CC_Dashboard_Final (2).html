Key Findings and Recommendations

Critical Risk

QA / Pre-processing delays account for R1,391 (66%) of total SLA breach cost. Five open breach events totalling R1,312 remain unresolved, representing active and growing financial exposure.

Root Cause

58% of all issues are people-driven, with training gaps identified as the primary root cause. This pattern repeats across QA, onboarding and front office — indicating a systemic L&D gap, not isolated incidents.

Recommended Actions

Priority 1: Structured coaching programme targeting QA processing SLAs. Priority 2: Process redesign for compliance documentation workflow. Priority 3: CRM enhancement to prevent admin queue misrouting.
