Key Findings and Recommendations

Q3 Spike — Root Cause

Late submissions nearly quadrupled from Q2 (11%) to Q3 (39%) — a 28-percentage-point deterioration. Root cause analysis identified FICA/Checklist errors and incomplete employer resolutions as primary drivers, accounting for the majority of all queried cases across both quarters.

Processing Time — Positive Trend

Despite the Q3 submission quality decline, average processing time improved consistently from 56.7 days in Q2 to 49.8 days in Q4. This suggests internal processing efficiencies improved even as submission quality from external parties declined.

Q4 Recovery — Action Effective

Q4 shows clear evidence of corrective action: SLA compliance reached 96%, cases over 60 days dropped from 25% to just 4%, and late submissions reduced from 39% to 28%. Recommended: formalise Q4 interventions as standard operating procedure.
